Desserts

Limed Grapes

    Ingredients
  • 500g White Grapes
  • 1x Lime
      • Steps
  • Wash the grapes and serve them on a cool looking bowl;
  • Grate the lime into the grapes, be sure to move them around so that it doesn't stay only on top;
  • Enjoy this delicious dessert with minimal effort!

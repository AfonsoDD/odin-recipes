Grilled pear with prosciutto

    Ingredients
  • 2 pears
  • Honey
  • Olive oil
  • Prosciutto
      • Steps
  • Cut the pears in quarters;
  • Set a fry to medium heat, add olive oil (just a tiny bit), add the honey;
  • Let the pears toast until they get a healthy goldish brown color;
  • Remove the pears and let them cool off;
  • Roll some prosciutto around every quarter of the pear;
  • Serve and enjoy!

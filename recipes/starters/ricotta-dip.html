Ricotta dip with lemon and capers

    Ingredients
  • 1x Ricotta chesse
  • Half a lemon
  • Capers
  • Olive oil
      • Steps
  • Set the ricotta in a container;
  • Squeeze the lemon juice onto the ricotta, add some salt and pepper together with olive oil (a tablespoon should be enough);
  • Whisk everything until desired texture. For additional softness I recommend adding either more lemon or olive oil, avoid water;
  • Serve gracefully (this is actually what my girlfriend told me to do);
  • Roast the capers with some olive oil at 180ÂºC for 15mins - you can use the oven, airfryer or even a pan;
  • Add the capers to the serving, finish with some spicy olive oil for maximum satisfaction.

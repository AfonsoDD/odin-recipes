Burrata with blood orange and kumato

    Ingredients
  • 1x Burrata
  • 1x Kumato
  • 1x Blood Orange
  • Hazelnuts
  • Basel
      • Steps
  • Cut the Kumato and Blood Orange into thin slices and plate them as you prefer;
  • Roast the hazelnuts;
  • Place Burrata above the thin slices;
  • In a recipient, mix olive oil, salt&pepper and the leftover juice of the Blood Orange;
  • Cover the burrata with the mix and hazelnuts;
  • Enjoy!
